# 中华民国 · 五权分立 / Republic of China · Five-Power Constitution

> 1912-1949 · Checks & Balances with Five-Branch Government

---

## 一、历史背景 / Historical Background

中华民国（1912-1949）是亚洲第一个民主共和国，由孙中山先生领导辛亥革命（1911年10月10日武昌起义）推翻清朝后建立。1912年1月1日，孙中山在南京就任临时大总统，宣告中华民国成立，结束了两千余年的帝制。这是中国政治史上最深刻的制度断裂——从皇权专制转向民主共和。

The Republic of China (1912-1949) was Asia's first democratic republic, established after Sun Yat-sen led the Xinhai Revolution (the Wuchang Uprising on October 10, 1911) to overthrow the Qing Dynasty. On January 1, 1912, Sun was inaugurated as Provisional President in Nanjing, proclaiming the Republic and ending over two millennia of imperial rule. This was the most profound institutional rupture in Chinese political history — from autocratic monarchy to democratic republic.

孙中山的政治理论体系以「三民主义」（民族、民权、民生）为纲，以「五权宪法」为核心制度设计。五权宪法是孙中山最具原创性的政治发明：在西方三权分立（行政、立法、司法）的基础上，增加了源自中国传统的考试权和监察权。孙中山认为，西方的三权分立存在两大缺陷：其一，官员任命容易沦为政党分赃（spoils system），需要独立的考试权来保证人才选拔的公正；其二，议会监察权往往受到政党利益干扰，需要独立的监察权来保证监督的有效性。

Sun Yat-sen's political theory system used the "Three Principles of the People" (Nationalism, Democracy, People's Livelihood) as its guiding principles, with the "Five-Power Constitution" as its core institutional design. The Five-Power Constitution was Sun's most original political invention: adding the Examination Power and Control (Supervisory) Power — drawn from Chinese tradition — to the Western tripartite separation of powers (executive, legislative, judicial). Sun argued that Western tripartite separation had two major flaws: first, official appointments easily devolved into party spoils systems, requiring independent examination power to ensure fair talent selection; second, parliamentary oversight was often compromised by partisan interests, requiring independent supervisory power for effective oversight.

民国初年（1912-1928）经历了袁世凯称帝（1915-1916）、军阀混战、北伐战争等动荡。1928年国民政府形式上统一全国后，进入「训政时期」——由国民党以党治国，为宪政做准备。1936年「五五宪草」起草但因抗日战争（1937-1945）搁置。抗战胜利后，1946年制宪国民大会在南京召开，通过《中华民国宪法》，正式确立五权分立体制。五院分别为：行政院（最高行政机关，院长由总统任命）、立法院（最高立法机关，委员民选）、司法院（最高司法机关，大法官释宪）、考试院（掌管公务员考试与铨叙）、监察院（掌管弹劾纠举与审计）。

The early Republic (1912-1928) experienced Yuan Shikai's ill-fated attempt to restore monarchy (1915-1916), warlord conflicts, and the Northern Expedition. After the Nationalist government nominally unified China in 1928, the "Political Tutelage" period began — the KMT governing to prepare for constitutional democracy. The 1936 draft constitution was shelved due to the Second Sino-Japanese War (1937-1945). After victory, the 1946 National Constituent Assembly in Nanjing passed the Constitution of the Republic of China, formally establishing the five-power system. The five Yuan (branches) are: Executive Yuan (highest administrative organ, premier appointed by president), Legislative Yuan (highest legislative organ, elected members), Judicial Yuan (highest judicial organ, grand justices for constitutional interpretation), Examination Yuan (civil service examinations and personnel management), and Control Yuan (impeachment, censure, and audit).

五权宪法体现了孙中山「以中国固有之考试监察二权，补西方三权之不足」的政治智慧。考试院承继了科举制度的精神——通过公开、公平的考试选拔人才，而非依靠政治关系。监察院则继承了御史台的传统——独立于行政和立法之外，专司弹劾纠举。国民大会作为「政权机关」，代表人民行使选举、罢免、创制、复决四权，与五院的「治权机关」形成人民主权与政府治理的分工。

The Five-Power Constitution embodied Sun's political wisdom of "supplementing Western tripartite separation with China's traditional examination and supervisory powers." The Examination Yuan inherited the spirit of the imperial examination system — selecting talent through open, fair examinations rather than political connections. The Control Yuan inherited the tradition of the Censorate — independent of executive and legislative branches, specializing in impeachment and censure. The National Assembly, as the "political power organ," represented the people in exercising the four powers of election, recall, initiative, and referendum, forming a division between popular sovereignty and governmental administration alongside the five Yuan as "governing organs."

## 二、制度设计详解 / System Design

### 核心架构 / Core Architecture

五权宪法的核心在于「权能区分」——人民有权（政权），政府有能（治权）。五院各司其职、互相制衡：行政院执行政策但受立法院预算审查和监察院弹劾制约；立法院制定法律但不能直接行政；司法院解释宪法但不能立法；考试院独立选拔人才不受政治干预；监察院独立监察不受行政控制。总统作为国家元首在五院之上统筹协调。

The Five-Power Constitution's core lies in the "distinction between power and ability" — the people have power (political rights), the government has ability (governing capacity). The five Yuan each have distinct functions with mutual checks: the Executive Yuan implements policy but is checked by Legislative Yuan budget review and Control Yuan impeachment; the Legislative Yuan legislates but cannot directly administer; the Judicial Yuan interprets the constitution but cannot legislate; the Examination Yuan independently selects talent free from political interference; the Control Yuan independently supervises free from executive control. The President, as head of state, coordinates above the five Yuan.

### 设计理念：从历史到 AI / Design Philosophy: From History to AI

五权宪法最独特的贡献在于考试院和监察院的设立。在 AI 编排中，考试院映射为独立的「测试验证」Agent——它不参与开发（行政院的职责），不制定标准（立法院的职责），专注于通过测试来验证交付物的质量。这种分离确保了测试的独立性和客观性，避免了「自己写代码自己测试」的弊端。

The Five-Power Constitution's most unique contribution is the establishment of the Examination and Control Yuan. In AI orchestration, the Examination Yuan maps to an independent "testing and verification" agent — it doesn't participate in development (Executive Yuan's role), doesn't set standards (Legislative Yuan's role), but focuses solely on verifying deliverable quality through testing. This separation ensures testing independence and objectivity, avoiding the pitfall of "testing your own code."

监察院映射为独立的「审计监察」Agent，它拥有弹劾权（指出问题）和审计权（检查资源使用），但没有行政权（不能直接修改代码）。这确保了监察的中立性。孙中山的「权能区分」理论在 AI 编排中表达为：用户（国民大会）拥有最终决策权（选什么、要什么），AI 五院拥有执行能力（怎么做、做多好），两者分工明确。

The Control Yuan maps to an independent "audit and oversight" agent with impeachment power (identifying problems) and audit power (checking resource usage) but no executive power (cannot directly modify code). This ensures oversight neutrality. Sun Yat-sen's "distinction between power and ability" theory is expressed in AI orchestration as: users (National Assembly) hold ultimate decision rights (what to select, what to want), while the AI five Yuan hold execution capability (how to do it, how well), with clear division of labor.

## 三、编排模式解析 / Orchestration Pattern

### 模式类型 / Pattern Type: Checks & Balances (五权制衡)

五院分权制衡、各司其职。行政执行、立法规范、司法裁决、考试把关、监察监督，形成完整的治理闭环。总统统筹但不直接干预各院内部事务。

### 信息流 / Information Flow

```
Step 1: 国民大会收集用户需求，提交总统
        National Assembly collects user requirements → President

Step 2: 总统审阅需求，转发行政院执行，同步通知其余四院
        President reviews, forwards to Executive Yuan
            │
            ├──→ 行政院：制定执行方案
            ├──→ 立法院：审查方案是否符合规范
            ├──→ 考试院：准备测试计划
            └──→ 监察院：记录审计线索

Step 3: 行政院制定方案，提交立法院审议
        Executive Yuan creates plan → Legislative Yuan reviews
        立法院: "预算超出标准20%，需削减" / "方案合规"

Step 4: 方案通过后执行，考试院同步准备测试
        Plan approved → Execution, Examination Yuan prepares tests

Step 5: 交付物提交考试院验证
        Deliverables → Examination Yuan verification
        考试院: "通过率 95%，3个关键用例失败"

Step 6: 监察院全程审计，出具报告
        Control Yuan audits throughout, issues report

Step 7: 争议或分歧 → 司法院仲裁
        Disputes → Judicial Yuan arbitrates

Step 8: 国民大会验收最终成果
        National Assembly accepts final deliverable
```

### 决策机制 / Decision Making

- **需求定义权**: 国民大会（用户代表）
- **执行权**: 行政院独享，其他四院不得直接执行
- **立法权**: 立法院制定规范和标准，行政院必须遵循
- **司法裁决权**: 当各院意见冲突时，司法院有最终裁决权
- **考试验证权**: 考试院独立测试，行政院不得干预测试过程
- **弹劾权**: 监察院可弹劾任何院的 Agent，要求整改
- **总统仲裁**: 五院之间的重大分歧，总统可介入协调

## 五、适用场景 / Best Use Cases

- **全生命周期软件项目 / Full-Lifecycle Software Projects**: 五院覆盖了从需求收集（国民大会）、规范制定（立法院）、开发执行（行政院）、测试验证（考试院）、审计监察（监察院）的完整生命周期。
- **QA 驱动的开发 / QA-Driven Development**: 考试院的独立测试机制确保质量不打折扣，适合对质量要求极高的项目。
- **多方利益相关者项目 / Multi-Stakeholder Projects**: 五院代表不同利益方，通过制度化的协商和制衡来达成共识。
- **合规密集型行业 / Compliance-Heavy Industries**: 监察院+司法院的双重合规保障，适合金融、医疗、政务等强监管领域。
- **AI 伦理审查 / AI Ethics Review**: 五权体系天然适合对 AI 输出进行多维度审查——技术可行性（行政院）、法规合规（立法院）、伦理审查（司法院）、质量测试（考试院）、审计追溯（监察院）。
- **教育培训平台 / Education & Training Platforms**: 考试院模式适合评估学习成果、认证能力等教育场景。
