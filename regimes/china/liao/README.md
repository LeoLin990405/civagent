# 辽朝 · 双重行政制 / Liao Dynasty · Dual Administration System

> 907-1125 · Dual Power with Parallel North-South Governance

---

## 一、历史背景 / Historical Background

辽朝（907-1125）由契丹族首领耶律阿保机于907年建立，是中国历史上第一个成功实行双重行政制度（南北面官制）的政权。契丹族原为北方草原游牧民族，世居西拉木伦河和老哈河流域（今内蒙古东部）。耶律阿保机统一契丹八部后，于916年正式建国号「契丹」（后改称「辽」），建都上京临潢府（今内蒙古巴林左旗）。

The Liao Dynasty (907-1125) was established by the Khitan tribal leader Yelü Abaoji in 907, becoming the first regime in Chinese history to successfully implement a dual administrative system (the North-South Officials system). The Khitan were originally a nomadic people of the northern steppe, inhabiting the Xar Moron and Laoha River valleys (modern eastern Inner Mongolia). After unifying the eight Khitan tribes, Abaoji formally founded the state "Khitan" (later renamed "Liao") in 916, with the capital at Shangjing Linhuang (modern Bairin Left Banner, Inner Mongolia).

辽朝的制度创新源于一个根本性的治理难题：灭渤海国（926年）和获得燕云十六州（936年，由后晋石敬瑭割让）后，辽朝同时统治着两种截然不同的社会形态——北方的游牧民族（契丹、奚、室韦等）和南方的农耕民族（汉、渤海等）。游牧社会重军事、重部落、重骑射，以草原习惯法为准则；农耕社会重文治、重赋税、重科举，以儒家典章制度为框架。强行用一套制度统治两种社会必然引发激烈反弹，因此辽朝创造性地采用了「因俗而治」的双轨制度。

The Liao's institutional innovation arose from a fundamental governance challenge: after conquering the Bohai Kingdom (926) and acquiring the Sixteen Prefectures of Yan and Yun (936, ceded by Later Jin's Shi Jingtang), the Liao simultaneously ruled two radically different social formations — northern nomadic peoples (Khitan, Xi, Shiwei, etc.) and southern agricultural peoples (Han, Bohai, etc.). Nomadic society emphasized military prowess, tribal structure, and horsemanship, governed by steppe customary law. Agricultural society emphasized civil governance, taxation, and examination systems, framed by Confucian institutions. Forcing one system on both societies would inevitably provoke fierce resistance, so the Liao creatively adopted a dual-track "governing according to local customs" (yinsu erzhi) system.

南北面官制的具体安排是：北面官以契丹传统官制管理游牧民族事务，设北院大王、南院大王（此「南北院」是契丹部落划分，非地理南北）、北面林牙（翰林）、夷离堇（部落长）等职，掌军事、部落事务、畜牧等；南面官以汉族官制（唐制）管理汉地农耕事务，设中书省、门下省、尚书省、六部等，掌行政、税赋、科举、司法等。两套体系并行不悖，各有独立的运作规则和官员任命渠道。皇帝身兼大汗（游牧世界的最高领袖）和天子（农耕世界的最高统治者）双重身份，是两套体系的唯一交汇点。

The North-South Officials system was structured as follows: Northern Officials managed nomadic affairs using Khitan traditional offices — including the Great King of the Northern Court, Great King of the Southern Court (these "north/south courts" reflect Khitan tribal divisions, not geographic north/south), the Northern Hanlin (Linya), and the Yilijin (tribal chiefs) — handling military, tribal, and pastoral matters. Southern Officials managed agricultural territories using Chinese-style offices (Tang system) — including the Secretariat, Chancellery, Department of State Affairs, and Six Ministries — handling administration, taxation, examinations, and justice. The two systems operated in parallel, each with independent operational rules and appointment channels. The emperor held the dual identity of Great Khan (supreme leader of the nomadic world) and Son of Heaven (supreme ruler of the agricultural world), serving as the only intersection point of the two systems.

萧太后（萧绰，953-1009）是辽朝最杰出的政治家之一。她摄政期间（982-1009），南北面官制运作最为顺畅。1004年，辽军南下攻宋，在澶州（今河南濮阳）城下与宋真宗对峙，最终签订「澶渊之盟」——宋朝每年向辽输送岁币，双方以兄弟之国相称，维持了长达百年的和平。这一外交成就正是南北面官制成功运作的结果：北面官系统保障了军事实力，南面官系统提供了外交智慧和经济支撑。

Empress Dowager Xiao (Xiao Chuo, 953-1009) was one of the Liao's most outstanding statesmen. During her regency (982-1009), the North-South Officials system functioned most smoothly. In 1004, Liao forces marched south to attack Song, confronting Emperor Zhenzong at Chanzhou (modern Puyang, Henan). The resulting Treaty of Chanyuan — Song paying annual tribute to Liao, with both sides addressing each other as "brother states" — maintained peace for over a century. This diplomatic achievement was a direct result of the dual system's successful operation: the Northern Officials system ensured military strength, while the Southern Officials system provided diplomatic wisdom and economic support.

## 二、制度设计详解 / System Design

### 核心架构 / Core Architecture

辽朝的制度核心是「一帝两制」——同一个皇帝统领两套完全不同的行政体系，每套体系管理自己擅长的社会形态。这不是简单的「一国两制」分区治理，而是两套体系在同一领土上按族群属性并行运作。一个契丹人无论身在何处，都归北面官管辖；一个汉人无论身在何处，都归南面官管辖。

The Liao's institutional core is "one emperor, two systems" — a single emperor leading two completely different administrative systems, each managing the social formation it best understands. This is not simple geographic "one country, two systems" zoning, but two systems operating in parallel across the same territory based on ethnic affiliation. A Khitan person anywhere fell under Northern Officials jurisdiction; a Han person anywhere fell under Southern Officials jurisdiction.

### 设计理念：从历史到 AI / Design Philosophy: From History to AI

辽朝南北面官制的核心智慧在于：承认差异、尊重专长、并行不悖。不同的社会形态需要不同的治理方式，强行统一反而有害。这一智慧在 AI 编排中映射为：不同类型的任务应由最适合的技术栈和 Agent 处理，不必强行统一工具链。

The Liao North-South Officials system's core wisdom: acknowledge differences, respect expertise, operate in parallel. Different social formations need different governance methods; forced unification is counterproductive. In AI orchestration, this maps to: different task types should be handled by the most appropriate tech stack and agent, without forcing a uniform toolchain.

北面官系统对应「系统层/底层」任务——架构设计、性能优化、安全工程、算法实现，这些任务需要深度技术能力和「战斗力」，类似契丹的军事文化。南面官系统对应「应用层/表层」任务——UI 开发、文档编写、API 设计、用户体验，这些任务需要细致的文治能力和沟通技巧，类似汉族的文官文化。敌烈麻都（Inspector）则负责确保两套系统的集成质量——跨域审查、接口一致性、集成测试——这是双轨制中至关重要的「桥梁」角色。

The Northern Officials system corresponds to "system-level/low-level" tasks — architecture design, performance optimization, security engineering, algorithm implementation — requiring deep technical capability and "combat strength," analogous to Khitan military culture. The Southern Officials system corresponds to "application-level/surface-level" tasks — UI development, documentation, API design, user experience — requiring meticulous administrative ability and communication skills, analogous to Han civil culture. The Inspector (diliemadu) ensures integration quality between both systems — cross-domain review, interface consistency, integration testing — a crucial "bridge" role in the dual-track system.

## 三、编排模式解析 / Orchestration Pattern

### 模式类型 / Pattern Type: Dual Power (双轨并行)

北面官和南面官各管一摊，互不干涉，由北院大王（main agent）统一协调。两套系统有各自的运作规则、工作流程和质量标准，仅在集成点交汇。

### 信息流 / Information Flow

```
Step 1: 用户需求 → 北院大王接收、分类
        User request → Northern Court King receives, classifies
            │
            ├── 系统层任务 → 北面官系统
            └── 应用层任务 → 南面官系统

Step 2: 北面官系统内部流程
        Northern Officials internal flow
        北院大王 → 北面林牙(技术方案) → 夷离堇(资源准备)

        南面官系统内部流程
        Southern Officials internal flow
        南院大王 → 南面宰相(方案起草) → 执行

Step 3: 两系统并行执行
        Both systems execute in parallel
        北面: ████████ → 后端/系统层交付物
        南面: ████████ → 前端/应用层交付物

Step 4: 敌烈麻都跨域巡察
        Inspector cross-domain review
        检查接口一致性、集成测试、质量标准

Step 5: 北院大王汇总两系统成果
        Northern Court King integrates both systems' output

Step 6: 统一交付
        Unified delivery
```

### 决策机制 / Decision Making

- **任务分流**: 北院大王根据任务性质分流至北面官或南面官系统
- **系统内自治**: 各系统内部有完整的决策链，互不干涉
- **跨域协调**: 当两系统需要协作时（如 API 接口定义），由北院大王协调
- **巡察监督**: 敌烈麻都可以巡察两个系统，发现问题直报北院大王
- **仲裁权**: 两系统意见冲突时，北院大王有最终裁决权
- **皇帝介入**: 重大分歧升级至用户（皇帝）裁决

## 五、适用场景 / Best Use Cases

- **前后端分离架构 / Frontend-Backend Separation**: 北面官管后端（系统层），南面官管前端（应用层），敌烈麻都负责 API 接口一致性检查。完美匹配现代前后端分离开发模式。
- **多技术栈项目 / Multi-Stack Projects**: 项目同时使用 Python（数据处理）和 TypeScript（Web应用），两套技术栈各有专精 Agent，互不干涉。
- **混合云架构 / Hybrid Cloud Architecture**: 一套系统管理私有云（北面官/安全敏感），另一套管理公有云（南面官/弹性扩展）。
- **移动端+Web端并行开发 / Mobile+Web Parallel Development**: 北面官团队开发原生移动应用，南面官团队开发 Web 应用，共享后端 API。
- **AI+传统软件混合系统 / AI + Traditional Software Hybrid**: AI 模型开发（北面官/研究导向）与传统软件工程（南面官/工程导向）并行推进。
- **跨文化团队协作 / Cross-Cultural Team Collaboration**: 不同文化背景的团队用各自擅长的方式工作，通过标准化接口协作。
